BatStateU - The NEU engineers sustainable future on WED 2023

Batangas State University, The National Engineering University (BatStateU-The NEU) is celebrating World Engineering Day (WED) for Sustainable Development 2023 on March 4,6,8, and 9. This hybrid event features diverse activities, including alumni series, workshops, a sustainable development forum, and academic competitions to showcase engineering students' abilities, expertise, and skills.
